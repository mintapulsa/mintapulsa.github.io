ABOUT US

Kinbi is a friendly, creative and innovative workspace located in Tangerang City, Banten. We provide seasonal and eventful themed workshops such as international celebrations, public holidays, and many more. Our eligible instructors came from all over the world and we promise to make you feel welcomed and at home in our space and sessions.

Everyday, we strive to create a skillful and creative community. We believe that our workshops not only open doors for people who’s looking for their passion but also for those who are starting out.

Learn to pick up new skills at Kinbi Workshop! Let yourself be curious, experimental and free as we will guide you throughout the process.
